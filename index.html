Our Story

Born at the 19th Hole

Salt & Fire Meat Co. lives in the clubhouse at Oak Knoll Golf Course in Ashland — where a day on the links ends with real smoked food and a cold drink.

We're butchers and cooks at heart. Everything starts with quality meat, a good rub of salt, and a whole lot of fire. Come hungry.
