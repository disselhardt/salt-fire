Weekly Meals & Pre-Orders

Ready-to-eat meals you order ahead, pay for online, and pick up at a set time. Real food for the busy week.

The Idea

Good Food Waiting For You

Each week we put together a rotating lineup of ready-to-eat meals — smoked, scratch-made, and portioned to take home.

Order by the deadline, choose your pickup time, pay online, and just swing by to grab it. No waiting in line, no wondering what's for dinner. Perfect for a busy week or stocking the fridge.
